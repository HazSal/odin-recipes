Description

A basic thick cheesecake, serve it with your favorite fruit topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, chopped walnuts, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a medium bowl; press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Remove from oven; cool.
  4. Beat cream cheese and white sugar together with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in sour cream and heavy cream; beat in flour and vanilla. Beat in eggs, one at a time, with mixer on low; pour batter over crust.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until center is almost set (filling will be soft), 60 to 70 minutes. Refrigerate at least 6 hours, or overnight, before removing from pan.
